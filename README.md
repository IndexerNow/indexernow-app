## What is IndexerNow?

IndexerNow pushes your URLs to Google, Bing & IndexNow in one click — then shows whether AI assistants can crawl, read, and cite your site. It uses your own Google Search Console account, so your data stays yours. No shared indexing pools. No subscription required to start.

**Live stats (real usage since May 25, 2026):**
- 40+ creators indexing
- 15,000+ URLs submitted
- 2,700+ pushed to Google
- 10,000+ pushed to Bing

## Features

### Indexing & Submission
- Bulk URL submission to **Google**, **Bing**, and **IndexNow** in one workflow
- Sitemap, RSS, and Webhook auto-push support
- Batch history and CSV export
- Uses your own Google Search Console permissions

### AI Search Visibility
- Real AI-crawler tracking (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended)
- `llms.txt` file validation and hosting
- 0–100 AI Discoverability Score
- AI citation and brand mention checker

### Auditing & SEO Health
- Indexation status per URL
- X-Robots-Tag, canonical, and robots.txt checks
- Indexability checker
- Schema markup inspector & generator
- SERP snippet preview, Open Graph preview
- Redirect chain checker, Hreflang tester
- Domain Rating checker

### Integrations
Works with: WordPress · Wix · Squarespace · Webflow · Shopify · Notion · Ghost · Framer
